Why We Built an Environmental Intelligence Platform Inside Excel

We could have built a standalone web app. We almost did. But when we watched environmental consultants work, the answer was obvious: they live in Excel. Their lab data arrives in spreadsheets, their reports get built in spreadsheets, and their clients expect spreadsheets. Asking them to learn a new platform is asking them to add friction, not remove it. So GRYD meets them where they are — automated exceedance flagging, guideline compliance, GIS mapping, and statistical analysis, all without ever leaving the workbook. Turns out the best tool adoption strategy is not making people adopt anything new at all.

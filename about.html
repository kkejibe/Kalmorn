About Kalmorn Tech

A Canadian technology company building software that makes a difference in healthcare and the environment.

Our Mission

At Kalmorn Tech, we believe technology should solve real-world problems. Our mission is to build innovative software products and deliver expert consulting that creates tangible impact in the industries we serve.

We focus on two domains where technology can truly transform outcomes: healthcare and the environment. Through our products and consulting practice, we help organizations embrace the future with confidence.

Our Vision

We envision a world where technology seamlessly bridges the gap between complex challenges and elegant solutions. Kalmorn Tech aims to be a leading force in developing purpose-driven software that enhances clinical workflows and advances environmental stewardship.

With ambitious product roadmaps and a growing consulting practice, we're positioned to scale our impact across Canada and beyond.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build and every partnership we form.

Innovation

We push boundaries and challenge conventions. Every product and solution we deliver reflects our commitment to creative, forward-thinking technology.

Quality

We take pride in craftsmanship. From code quality to user experience, we hold ourselves to the highest standards in everything we ship.

Impact

Technology for technology's sake isn't our style. We measure success by the real-world difference our work makes in people's lives and the environment.
